Marmalade drawn in a calico pattern, posing like a Jojo character, with the symbols for "menacing" around her.

## The Story

I was recovering from a surgery at the time, and was chatting with ChatGPT, and got this idea of Marmalade (Gothfield back then), making a Jojo pose. I asked it to generate a 3D simple model pose reference so I can use it (which you can see in the .clip file), rather than me going to the PC and turning on Clip Studio Paint just to make a 3D reference from the built in one there, as that was so much work. I then took the pose reference, and used it, and this is what happened.
