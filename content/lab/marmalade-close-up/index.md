A very cute Marmalade getting all up in your face, wondering what you're doing.

## The Story

This was created as a design on the loyalty card for the system that [chuk_chan](https://www.twitch.tv/chuk_chan) uses for his stream. The previous [one](../marmalade-rock) was a more normal design. This one started as her face only, and then midway I realised that this would be perfect as a "camera" shot, with the 4 lines framing her.
