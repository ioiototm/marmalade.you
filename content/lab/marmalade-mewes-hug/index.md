A cute drawing of Marmalade (based on the original design from the [Marmalade Origin](https://marmalade.you/lab/marmalade-origin/) post) hugging Mewes, a rat/mouse CC0 mascot from [here](https://mewes-creativity.github.io/).

## The Story
I discovered another CC0 mascot, Mewes, and it got me inspired to draw them together, as CC0 buddies. I wanted to draw them hugging, because why not, and it's even funnier as Mal is a cat, and Mewes is a rat/mouse - they should be enemies, but they're not. I tried my best to make Mewes look like the original design, but it's difficult (for me). The hugging arms were a PAIN to draw, I had to redraw them multiple times - you can see most of the tries in the .clip file.
