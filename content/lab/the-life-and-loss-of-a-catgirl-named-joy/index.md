This is a movie poster of a fictional movie, as the title suggests, starring Marmalade as Joy. 

## The Story

Somehow through my chats with ChatGPT back then, we jokingly came up with the idea of that movie, "The Life and Loss of a Catgirl Named Joy". I thought it would be fun to make a movie poster for it, so I did! A lot of small easter eggs in the credits on the poster, tried a few pastel things for fur texture, tried calico as a thing, and no, just no, so much work. Back then I was trying to make Calico Gothfield a thing, but it's just so much work to make the pattern look good.
